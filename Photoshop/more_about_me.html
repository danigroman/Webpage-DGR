My Inspiration

I am very close to my mother, Ingrid, and have been working with her for the past year.
I was born when she was very young, and many of the experiences I've had are thanks to her support.
She immigrated just before her college years, yet managed to earn a scholarship and, while pregnant, completed her bachelor's, master's, doctorate, and second master's degree.
She is a strong influence in my life and my greatest support.
My decision to enroll at NYU was partly inspired by her teenage dream of attending that university, a dream that influenced me from a young age.
